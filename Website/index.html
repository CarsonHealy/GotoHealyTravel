Intro

Welcome to my website about Turkey! Here, you will find information about the country's rich culture, history, and some of the best places to visit. Turkey is a country that bridges Europe and Asia.

About Turkey!

Turkey is a transcontinental country located mainly on the Anatolian Peninsula in Western Asia, with a smaller portion on the Balkan Peninsula in Southeast Europe. It is bordered by eight countries and has coastlines along the Aegean Sea, the Mediterranean Sea, and the Black Sea. The country has a rich history that dates back to ancient times, with influences from various civilizations including the Hittites, Phrygians, Urartians, Lydians, and more.

Food

Turkish cuisine is known for its rich flavors and diverse dishes. Some of my favourite include:

  • Mezes (appetizers)
  • Börek (savory pastries)
  • Baklava (sweet pastry)
  • Turkish tea and coffee
  • Gözleme(Turkish Pancake-Inside can be tasty spinach and poatao)

Culture

Turkey has a rich cultural heritage that reflects its history and diverse influences. The country is known for its traditional music, dance, and art. Turkish carpets and ceramics are also highly regarded for their intricate designs and craftsmanship.

Turkish hospitality is famous, and visitors are often welcomed with open arms.The concept of "çay" (tea) culture is an part of social life, where sharing tea with friends and family is a tradition.

History

Turkey has a long and complex history, with influences from various civilizations and many empires.


Some of thoose civilizations include:
